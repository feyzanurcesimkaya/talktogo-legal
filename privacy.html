Privacy Policy

Last updated: DATE_HERE

TalkToGo is a voice-first place recommendation and navigation assistant. This policy explains what data we collect and how we use it.

1) Data We Collect

  • Account info (optional): email address if you create an account.
  • Location data (optional): latitude/longitude only when you allow location permission, used to recommend nearby places and create routes.
  • Voice input (optional): if you use voice recognition, speech is converted to text by your device/browser. TalkToGo receives the text you submit.
  • Chat content: messages you send to get recommendations.
  • Technical data: basic logs for security and debugging (e.g., timestamps, browser type).

2) How We Use Your Data

  • Provide recommendations and navigation links.
  • Improve results and personalization (if enabled in settings).
  • Maintain security and prevent abuse.

3) Sharing of Data

We may send your query and location to third-party services to provide functionality:

  • Google Maps / Places / Directions to fetch places and routes.
  • AI service provider to understand intent and generate replies.
  • TikTok only if you connect your TikTok account (OAuth) to fetch trending content.

We do not sell your personal data.

4) Data Retention

  • Conversation context may be stored temporarily to support follow-up questions.
  • Favorites and saved locations are stored for your account if you use those features.
  • You can reset preferences and conversation from the app settings.

5) Your Choices

  • You can use TalkToGo in guest mode.
  • You can disable location access in your browser settings.
  • You can disable personalization in the app settings.
